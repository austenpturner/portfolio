About Me

I'm a Full Stack Web Developer based in Seattle, WA. I build and design websites and apps. My projects combine a polished and responsive user interface with a fast and efficient server. I prioritize writing clean, organized and well-documented code that lends to user-friendly applications.

My education includes a Certificate in Full-Stack Web Development from the University of Washington and a Bachelor of Science in Psychology from Western Washington University. I have strong background in customer service, working with diverse populations, and am expereinced collaborating with a web development team to build and deploy applications. Often described as discliplined and motivated, I am always working to expand my skill set and am continually learning new languages and technologies. When not coding I enjoy board games and exploring the PNW.
